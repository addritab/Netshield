Password Security

Strong passwords are necessary in todayâ€™s digital account-driven world where everything, including our most sensitive information, is online. Strong passwords help keep your personal information safe, protect emails and files, and prevent someone from getting into your account.

Strong passwords are passwords that are not easy to guess or crack.

Strong passwords have:

  • At least 12 characters
  • Upper and lower-case letters
  • numbers
  • special characters
  • Does not use personal information
  • A unique password for every account

A weak password is a combination of characters or words that is easy for anyone to guess.

Weak passwords are:

  • Short passwords
  • Recognizable keystroke patterns like "qwerty
  • Including personal information
  • Common passwords like password123
  • Repeated letters or numbers
